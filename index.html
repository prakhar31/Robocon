About Us

We are a group of enthusiasts who design and build robots to compete in ABU Robocon, which is one of the biggest collegiate level robotics competition globally. The nationals are held in Pune each year. The Asia-Pacific Robot Contest (ABU Robocon) is an Asian Oceanian College robot competition, founded in 2002 by Asia-Pacific Broadcasting Union. In the competition robots compete to complete a task within a set period of time. The contest aims to create friendship among young people with similar interests who will lead their countries in the 21st century, as well as help advance engineering and broadcasting technologies in the region. The event is broadcast in many countries through ABU member broadcasters.

Workshop

The Team organizes Workshops in colleges in and around Pilani. Over the years the Team has conducted workshops on Object following,Edge avoiding,Line following, Obstacle avoiding and Gesture Controlled Robots besides others.All the workshops start with basic level robotics before going to the details.Basics of Programming and Electronics are also taught and sufficient practice given.

Newsletter

The Team releases a monthly newsletter for the students of BITS Pilani .The newsletter contains various project ideas with step by step guide to them.It also contains information about recent advancements in the field of robotics.

Inorder to read our newsletters, you must login to the website.

Competetions

Team Robocon represents BITS Pilani in "ABU ROBOCON" held annually. It is an international Robotics Contest whose nationals are held in Pune.The problem statement involves building robots for accomplishing various challenging tasks which involve innovation in the fields of Design,Manufacturing,Electronics and Programming.The bots developed are either semi autonomous or fully autonomous. Besides this, The Team takes part in various Robotic Competitions held across the country during various times of the year. All the bots developed by the Team are put to display in Apogee, the annual technical festival of BITS,Pilani.
